Technology

Why Fuel Cell matters.

Not all alcohol sensors are equal. The technology inside the device determines the reliability of every result.

AF-50AD · Fuel Cell

Electrochemical Fuel Cell

  • ✓ Detects only alcohol (ethanol) — zero false positives from acetone, perfume or food
  • ✓ Same technology used by law enforcement & professional workplace testing worldwide
  • ✓ Consistent accuracy across the full temperature range (-5°C to +50°C)
  • ✓ Results legally admissible — certified to EN 15964 metrological standards
  • ✓ Warm-up in under 5 seconds, ready for continuous fleet-scale testing
Standard devices

Semiconductor Sensor

  • ✗ Reacts to ketones, acetone & other substances — produces false positives in diabetic workers
  • ✗ Consumer/recreational grade — not accepted in professional compliance contexts
  • ✗ Accuracy degrades in extreme heat or cold — unreliable on outdoor industrial sites
  • ✗ Results not legally admissible — cannot be used in regulatory proceedings
  • ✗ No calibration standard — measurement drift over time cannot be detected
Device Components

Every part engineered for the field.

Mouthpiece Slot
Accepts single-use hygienic mouthpieces. Each employee uses a new, individually sealed mouthpiece — ensuring hygiene and preventing cross-contamination on shared devices.
OLED Display
High-contrast 128×64px OLED screen displays BAC value, test number, time and battery level. Readable in full sunlight on outdoor industrial sites and in low-light underground environments.
Bluetooth 5.0
Pairs instantly with the Sobber mobile app via Bluetooth 5.0. Test results are transmitted in real time to the cloud dashboard — no manual data entry, no risk of result manipulation.
Status LED
Colour-coded status indicator visible from a distance. Green for negative, red for positive, amber for calibration due. Supervisors can confirm test status at a glance across a busy site entrance.
Power Button
Hold 2 seconds to power on. Device auto-warms in under 5 seconds. Auto-powers off after 3 minutes of inactivity — preserving the 1,500-test battery life across full operational days.
AA Battery Cover
Runs on standard 2× AA alkaline batteries — available worldwide, no charging infrastructure required. Critical advantage for remote mines and offshore sites with no reliable power supply.
Calibration & Service
