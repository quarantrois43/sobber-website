How It Works

Your peace of mind in 3 steps

A complete, verified alcohol test in under 3 minutes. Simple for the worker. Tamper-proof for the organisation.

Step 01 — Identity
AI Facial Recognition

The Sobber app activates the camera. AI verifies identity against the enrolled biometric profile in real time. No badge, no PIN — just a face. Proxy testing: impossible.

Step 02 — Measurement
Certified BAC Reading

The worker blows into the AF-50AD. The Platinum Fuel Cell delivers a certified result in under 5 seconds — ±0.005 %BAC precision. The same standard used by law enforcement.

Step 03 — Results
Instant Reporting

Results land on the sobber.app dashboard instantly — GPS-tagged, face-verified, permanently logged. Positive results trigger immediate SMS/email to supervisors.
