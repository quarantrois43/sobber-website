How It Works

Your peace of mind in 3 steps

Simplicity and reliability combined. Complete a full, verified alcohol test in under 3 minutes.

Step 01 — Identity
Facial Recognition

The Sobber app activates the front camera. AI verifies the employee's identity in real time by comparing the live image to the enrolled profile. No badge, no PIN — just a face.

Step 02 — Measurement
Precise BAC Reading

The worker blows steadily into the AF-50AD mouthpiece. The premium Platinum Fuel Cell sensor captures the breath sample and delivers a result with ±0.005 %BAC precision in under 5 seconds.

Step 03 — Results
Instant Reporting

Results appear immediately in the sobber.app dashboard — timestamped, GPS-tagged, linked to a verified face. Alerts are sent instantly to site supervisors by SMS or email. The test is logged permanently.
