Metrological Standards

The AF-50AD is calibrated according to EN 15964 — the European standard for professional breath alcohol testing instruments. Results meet the evidentiary requirements of regulatory audits, insurance investigations and internal HSE procedures.

Calibration recommended every 12 months or 1,000 tests. Device tracks and alerts automatically.

Data Sovereignty

EN 15964 is the European metrological standard for professional breath alcohol testing instruments. It defines accuracy requirements, calibration protocols and operating conditions. A device certified to this standard produces results that are recognised by regulatory bodies, insurers and occupational health authorities across the EU and in many jurisdictions globally.
