Enterprise Features

Everything your safety team needs.

AI Identity Vault

Every test is cross-referenced with facial biometrics to eliminate proxy testing and fraud. The enrolled profile adapts over time — just like your phone's Face ID.

Employee biometric data is encrypted and stored separately from test results. Access is role-gated: only designated HR or compliance officers can view facial images.

Automated Alerts

Receive instant SMS or email notifications the moment a positive BAC is detected on any site. Configurable alert recipients by site, team or result level.

A second alert is sent if a missed test window expires — ensuring supervisors are notified of non-compliance as well as positive results.

Smart Reporting

Generate audit-ready reports for insurance and regulatory bodies in seconds. Export individual test records or site-wide compliance summaries in PDF or CSV format.

Monthly compliance reports are generated automatically and sent to designated administrators — ready for insurance renewal or regulatory submission.

Geofencing & GPS

Track the exact GPS coordinates of every test to ensure compliance across multiple remote job sites. Geofencing alerts flag tests taken outside the designated site boundary.

For transport and logistics, pre-departure GPS tagging proves the test was taken at the right location before the vehicle departed — admissible in road incident investigations.
